Who We Are

Precision in
every line.

Vivikisha was founded on a singular conviction: that truly great design is invisible. It doesn't announce itself — it simply makes life better, quieter, and more beautiful.

We are a boutique architecture and interior design practice based in Bengaluru. Our work spans private residences, boutique hospitality, and considered commercial environments. Every project begins with deep listening, and ends with a space that feels unmistakably right.
